Why Donate Blood?

Your one donation can save up to 3 lives. Here's why it matters:

Save Lives

Every 2 seconds someone needs blood. Your donation makes a difference instantly.

Support Community

Donating blood helps hospitals maintain a steady supply for emergencies and surgeries.

Rare Blood Types

People with rare blood groups depend on voluntary donors like you to survive.

Health Benefits

Regular donation improves blood circulation and can even lower risk of heart disease.
